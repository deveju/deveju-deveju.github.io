➤ To play the game, just click on the buttons when they appear on screen.
➤ You have 60 seconds to score the highest you can.
➤ Clicking a button will give you +1 score.
➤ Missing a button will give you -1 score.
➤ If your score reaches less than zero, you lose.
➤ When the timer reaches 0, your score will appear on screen.
